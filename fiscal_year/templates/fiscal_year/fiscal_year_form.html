Help & Guidelines
Fiscal Year Guidelines:
  • Fiscal years should not overlap with each other
  • End date must be after start date
  • Only one fiscal year can be current at a time
  • Use clear, descriptive names for easy identification
Common Fiscal Year Patterns:
  • Calendar Year: Jan 1 - Dec 31
  • Academic Year: Jul 1 - Jun 30
  • Financial Year: Apr 1 - Mar 31
  • Custom: Any 12-month period
