How it works:
  1. Select customer(s) and storage period
  2. System analyzes storage logs for the period
  3. Applies customer-specific charge rates
  4. Generates invoice with calculated totals
  5. Invoice is created in "Draft" status
Charge Types:
  • Per Pallet/Day: Fixed rate per pallet per day
  • Per CBM/Day: Rate based on volume per day
  • Per Item/Day: Rate based on item quantity per day
  • Per Weight/Day: Rate based on weight per day
  • Fixed Monthly: Fixed monthly rate
