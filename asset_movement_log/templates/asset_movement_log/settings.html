Settings Information

Configure how asset movements are handled in your system.

Setting Descriptions:
  • Require Approval: All movements must be approved before they can be completed.
  • Auto-approve Assignments: Asset assignments are automatically approved.
  • Require Reason: A reason must be provided for all movements.
  • Max Duration Days: Maximum allowed duration for movements in days.
  • Enable Notifications: Send email notifications for movements.
Tips:
  • Enable approval for better control over asset movements
  • Set reasonable duration limits to prevent overdue movements
  • Use notifications to keep stakeholders informed
