Category Information
Assets

Resources owned by the business that have economic value.

  • Current Assets (cash, inventory, receivables)
  • Fixed Assets (equipment, buildings, vehicles)
  • Intangible Assets (patents, trademarks, goodwill)
Liabilities

Obligations and debts owed by the business.

  • Current Liabilities (accounts payable, short-term loans)
  • Long-term Liabilities (mortgages, bonds)
  • Provisions (estimated future obligations)
Equity

Owner's investment and retained earnings.

  • Share Capital
  • Retained Earnings
  • Reserves
Revenue

Income generated from business activities.

  • Operating Revenue
  • Other Revenue
  • Interest Income
Expenses

Costs incurred in running the business.

  • Operating Expenses
  • Administrative Expenses
  • Financial Expenses
